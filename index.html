Our Services

Ocean Freight

From the moment you register your shipment at Taymyr Forwarding we will handle every aspect of the process. Weather your shipments are entirely or part ocean freight, we will track and manage them ensuring a safe and timely delivery. With our extensive network, we handle various shipment sizes from single pallets to full trailer loads. We also deal with the local dispatch ensuring your shipment is delivered to its final destination. Whether a straightforward delivery or more complex custom shipment, our transport solutions are always designed in a cost effective way to meet your needs.

Air Freight

The majority of the transports that we can provide for our customers take place through sea freight. A reliable solution with varying possibilities. Taymyr Forwarding also offers airfreight solutions. Our experts can help you further, where more speed is desired to get the shipment to place of destination. Our network offers many options and we have access to a large number of flights from various airlines. Of course we also take care of the usual formalities surrounding the airports. A transport process from door to door, anywhere in the world!

Rail Freight

When making a choice, all transport possibilities are mapped, looking at costs, urgency and efficiency. About Taymyr

Taymyr Peninsula

The Taymyr Peninsula is a peninsula in the Far North of Russia, in the Siberian Federal District, that forms the northernmost part of the mainland of Eurasia. It lies between the Yenisei Gulf of the Kara Sea and the Khatanga Gulf of the Laptev Sea in Krasnoyarsk Krai, Russia. The region spans an area of over 400,000 km2 and is the most northern part of the continent. MMC Norilsk Nickel conducts mining operations in the area. The company conducts smelting operations in the area of the city of Norilsk, near the peninsula. The nickel ore concentrate and other products of the company are transported over a short railroad to the port city of Dudinka lied on the Yenisei River, and from there by seagoing vessels to Murmansk and other ports of the world.

Taymyr Forwarding

Taymyr Forwarding specializes in the transportation of cargo to and from Russia, especially to the Tajmyr Peninsula of Siberia. A regular, oversized or special transport to or from this part of the world is not always straightforward and the local handling often requires a unique approach. Of course, together with our extensive network, Taymyr Forwarding also provides delivery solutions to all regions of the world, taking care of your transportation needs professionally and efficiently. Throughout the entire process, we will ensure you are fully informed of the progress. We understand the importance of logistics and will communicate and manage any changes to the shipment details to you immediately.
